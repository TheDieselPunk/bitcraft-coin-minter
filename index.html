⚙ Bitcraft Coin Minter

Pulls traveler tasks via the Bitjita API — shows item IDs, trader stock, and market prices.

Install

1

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible in your browser (Ctrl+Shift+B in Chrome/Edge).

2

Drag the button below onto your bookmarks bar.

Usage

3

Navigate to any Bitjita page for your player — your profile, the market, anywhere that has your player ID in the URL.
On the first run you'll be prompted for your Claim Entity ID (from the claim URL). It's saved automatically for future runs.

4

Click ⚙ Coin Minter in your bookmarks bar. A table of all incomplete tasks across all travelers will appear — with trader stock and market data filled in within a few seconds.
